# PCM -- [ Pokemon Card Manager ]

---

An application to manage your **Pokemon Cards** collection.
- **Manage Your Pokémon Card Collection:** Easily catalog and categorize your Pokémon cards to keep an organized record.

- **Track Card Values:** Monitor the value of your cards over time and visualize their price history with intuitive charts.

- **Create Wishlists:** Build lists of cards you desire and receive alerts for price fluctuations, helping you make informed purchase decisions.

- **Efficient Card Discovery:** Quickly find cards using a diverse range of filters, or explore by categories or sets for a seamless browsing experience.

---

 Built using: **`C#`**, **`.NET 7.0`**, **`Cefsharp`**, **`SQLite`**, **`Blazor Hybrid`**, **`ASP.NET`** 
 
 [Pokemon Cards V2 css](https://poke-holo.simey.me/)  and the [Pokemon TCG API](https://pokemontcg.io/).
